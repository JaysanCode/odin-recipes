Spinach Alfredo Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Spinach
  • Alredo sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese

Preparations

  1. Fling dough in air till it becomes a large 16 inch pizza
  2. Toss your toppings evenly onto the pizza in your desired manner
  3. Lift the pizza with a pizza stick and slide it in the pizza oven
  4. Cook pizza in brick oven till it's good
  5. Profit.
