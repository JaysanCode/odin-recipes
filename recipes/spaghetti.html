Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Pasta
  • Marinaria
  • Ground Meat
  • Egg
  • Rosemary

Preparations

  1. Boil water on the stovetop
  2. Put pasta in boiling water, wait for it to become "al-dente"
  3. In another pan, cook meat till well done
  4. In a bowl, mixed the drained pasta, marina, and meat together and mix till it becomes cheese
  5. Profit.
